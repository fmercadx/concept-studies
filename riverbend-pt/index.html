Before Your First Visit

Questions patients ask before they call

General information only — your evaluation covers your specific situation.

Do I really not need a referral?

Correct — our therapists are Direct Access Certified, so you can be evaluated and begin treatment without a physician's prescription. Some insurance plans have their own referral rules, and we'll help you check yours when you call.

What should I expect at my first appointment?

A one-on-one evaluation with a licensed physical therapist: a conversation about your history and goals, a movement and strength assessment, and a clear explanation of your plan of care before you leave.

Do you take my insurance?

We work with most major insurance plans. Call either location with your card handy and we'll verify your coverage and explain any costs before your first visit.

What should I wear or bring?

Comfortable clothing you can move in, your insurance card, and a photo ID. If you have imaging or physician notes, bring them — helpful, but not required.
